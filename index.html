About Me

I have been working in IT for about 30 years, started out as a data entry operator and gradually moved up to be a programmer, systems analyst and project manager. I had the unique opportunity to work as an in-house IT manager as well as consultant for diversed vertical industry segments such as, banking, insurance, ecommerce, mining and consulting practices for IBM, Unimin, Standard Charterd Bank, Reckitt & Colman, Swiss Re etc., using programming in Basic & COBOL and gradually migrated to FoxPro, DB2, PowerBuilder, Visual Basic etc. In addition, I had the opportunity to learn Lotus Notes and eventually when the Notes Domino was launched I began working on HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Java Aplets. During the past 5-6 years I have been working primarily as a project manager involving client facing roles and felt a bit outdated with my technical skills causing me to join the coding Bootcamp at Columbia Engineering School.

In my personal life I enjoy cooking, tasting wine and listening to classical music. Few years ago I went to Israel to visit my wife's family and attended a traditional Jewish wedding. It is amazing how some of the wedding rituals are same as Hindu wedding rituals.
